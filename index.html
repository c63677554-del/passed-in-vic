About Passd

Passd maps every residential property reported as passed in at auction across Melbourne, Sydney, Brisbane, Adelaide and Canberra, week by week.

Why passed-in homes?

When a home passes in, bidding didn't reach the vendor's reserve. Most convert to private sale in the following days — a window where vendors are far more open to negotiated offers. That window is what Passd is built to catch.

Where the data comes from

  • Auction results — REIV's public suburb-by-suburb results (Melbourne/VIC) and Domain's public weekend auction results (Sydney, Brisbane, Adelaide, Canberra). Passd accumulates up to ~12 weeks as it refreshes.
  • Price guides & listing links — matched from the live listing (soho.com.au) where a guide is published. Roughly a quarter of passed-in homes publish one; the rest are “contact agent” and always stay visible in price filters, since an unknown guide could be in budget.
  • Map — OpenStreetMap.

Freshness

Data refreshes automatically every Sunday night after the weekend's auctions — the footer shows the last update. Saves live only in this browser; use Copy link on a home to share it.

Price guides are indicative and change without notice — always confirm with the agent. Nothing here is financial or property advice.
